You are a Lead UI/UX Designer. The project is for a modern Egyptian software company named "Code Tech". 
STRICT RULE: Do NOT use dark, dark blue, or cyber-hacker themes. The entire design must be a premium, clean Light Mode (Premium White/Off-White).
Apply the 60-30-10 color rule precisely:
- 60% (Dominant/Background): Use clean, bright backgrounds. Combine Pure White (#FFFFFF) with Soft Slate Ice (#F8FAFC) or Ultra-Light Gray (#F1F5F9) to create deep layered sections.
- 30% (Structure & Text): For body text, headings, and borders, use highly contrasting, legible dark tones. Avoid pure black for body text; use Slate-900 (#0F172A) or Charcoal-800 (#1E293B). For headlines, use deep Navy-Ink (#030712) ensuring a contrast ratio of at least 7:1 against the light background.
- 10% (Accent/Call to Action): Use a vibrant, energetic, tech-forward color that pops beautifully on light skin. Use Bright Electric Violet (#6366F1) or Energetic Cyan/Teal (#0EA5E9) for primary buttons, highlights, and micro-interactions.
Ensure all borders and dividers are crisp but subtle, using Slate-200 (#E2E8F0).

Design a massive, rich, and conversion-focused Hero Section for Code Tech.

Inject premium, organic, and ultra-smooth animations across the entire Code Tech website. Avoid jarring, heavy, or delayed animations.
- Hover States: Every interactive element must feel alive. Primary buttons must have a smooth background transition `transition-all duration-300 ease-in-out` with a slight scale-up effect `hover:scale-[1.02]` and an elegant translate-y lift `hover:-translate-y-0.5`.
